Super Crispy Homemade Onion Rings

One of my all time favorite fast foods are onions rings. I am, however, fairly fussy in terms of how they are made and how they taste. In my opinion, they need to be super crispy, have some spice, and heat. Many onion rings I have had just do not come up to my standards. In light of this, I decided to make my own recipe. Believe me, these onion rings are super crispy and delicious. Give the recipe a try, and let us know what you think.



Here are the ingredients need for making onion rings. Exact measuremnts of the recipe card below:

  • Large white onion sliced into 1/4 inch strips and divided into rings
  • 3/4 Cup of all purpose flour
  • 1/4 Cup of corn starch
  • 1/4 Tsp of cayenne pepper
  • Ground black pepper and salt to taste
  • 1 Cup of cold sparkling water or plain water
  • 1 or 2 Cups of Panko bread crumbs or the bread crumbs of your choice.
  • Oil for frying
